About Chess Champions

Chess Champions is an elementary school chess club based out of Marin County specializing in tutoring kids from K-5th. Meeting time and locations are currently being scheduled. Until further notice

About Our Team

Our team at Chess Champions is made up of enthusiastic highschoolers who aim to improve others and themselves. If you wish to join our team and have expience playing chess, feel free to contact us to join us in our next meeting

Stanley Ho: Founder of Chess Champions

Hey there! My name is Stanley and I am currently a freshman at Marin Catholic High School. When I'm not playing chess, I am probably either playing videogames or playing sports with my friends. I was introduced to playing chess by my dad in first grade and have been playing actively throughout my life. I got the idea of Chess Champions after volunteering at Saint Isabella's Chess Club and was inspired to go out into my community and teach young kids about this wonderful game.
