Teaching Methods

At Chess Champions, we believe in a hands on approach to help kids understand chess. Typically, we will start off our meetings by solving a simple chess puzzles together while also going over a new chess opening. After our warmup, kids will be paired off with an opponent of a similar skill level in order to improve their gameplay. In chess, we learn through others.

Beginners

No person can ever be too late to learn how to play chess. Chess is a sport which can be played and understood by anyone in the world. With newcomers to chess, our instructors will personally tutor and teach the basic moves of each piece. After getting used to how each piece functions, our instructors will go through a slow and guided game with your child. Chess, at its core is a simple game that can lead to complex results.
